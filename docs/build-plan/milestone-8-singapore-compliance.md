# Milestone 8 – Success Metrics & Commercialisation Strategy

## Objective

Define how project success will be measured and how the platform may eventually create commercial value.

The objective is not immediate monetisation.

The objective is to ensure the platform is solving a meaningful problem and creating measurable value.

---

## Core Problem Being Solved

Financial statement preparation remains highly manual across many small and medium-sized enterprises.

Common challenges include:

* Trial balance cleansing
* Financial statement mapping
* Disclosure preparation
* Audit support documentation
* Repetitive annual processes

These activities consume significant professional time despite being highly structured.

The platform seeks to automate these workflows while maintaining audit readiness and compliance with Singapore financial reporting requirements.

---

## Success Metrics

### Phase 1 Success

Trial Balance Ingestion

Success Criteria:

* Upload Excel trial balance
* Validate structure automatically
* Detect missing balances
* Generate standardised data output

Target:

* Reduce manual data preparation by 90%

---

### Phase 2 Success

Mapping Engine

Success Criteria:

* Map accounts automatically
* Support configurable mappings
* Generate audit trail of mapping decisions

Target:

* Reduce mapping effort by 80%

---

### Phase 3 Success

Financial Statement Generator

### Phase 4 Success

Notes Automation

### Phase 5 Success

## Competitive Differentiation

The moat is not account mappings.

Mappings can be recreated.

The moat is the integration of:

* Data ingestion
* Mapping workflows
* Statement generation
* Notes automation
* Audit evidence generation
* AI-assisted reporting

into a single workflow.

The platform focuses on automation of the complete financial reporting lifecycle.

---

## Success Definition

The project is successful if:

1. Financial statements can be generated with minimal human intervention.

2. Outputs are sufficiently structured to support audit review.

3. Preparation time is materially reduced.

4. Reporting quality remains consistent.

5. The platform can support multiple companies with minimal customization.
